# Project Overview

Turkey Manufacturers Platform is a B2B sourcing marketplace designed to connect
Turkish manufacturers and exporters with international buyers.

## Goals
- Promote Turkish manufacturers globally
- Generate RFQ leads
- Build a scalable B2B platform
- Monetize through supplier subscriptions

## Users

### Buyers
International companies searching for Turkish suppliers.

### Suppliers
Turkish manufacturers showcasing products and receiving RFQs.

### Admin
Platform operators managing suppliers, products, subscriptions and RFQs.
